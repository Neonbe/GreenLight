What does "developer cannot be verified" mean?

Errors like "macOS cannot verify that this app is free from malware" or "unidentified developer" appear when an app lacks Apple notarization. This is macOS Gatekeeper being cautious — the developer hasn't registered with Apple's Developer Program, so macOS can't verify their identity. It does not mean the app is malicious. Most indie apps, open-source tools, and software distributed outside the Mac App Store trigger this warning.
